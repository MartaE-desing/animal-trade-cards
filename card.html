Tartigrade

Tardigrades are microscopic eight-legged animals that have been to outer space and would likely survive the apocalypse. Bonus: They look like adorable miniature bears. They are not so funny.

  • Diet: Omnivore
  • Size: 0,5 mm
  • Habitat: Everywhere
  • Survival trick: Cryptobiosis

Tardigrades belong to an elite category of animals known as extremophiles, or critters that can survive environments that most others can't. For instance, tardigrades can go up to 30 years without food or water.
